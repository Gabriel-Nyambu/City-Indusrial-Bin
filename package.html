    Day 1

    Lake Nakuru National Park

    After breakfast you’ll drive past extinct volcano of mt longonot and suswa to the lake Nakuru national park situated on the floor of the Rift Valley. The park is notorious for its breathtaking panoramic view of the lake and surrounding escapements, has largest number flamingos and diverse species of birds and wildlife.
    Check in at lake Nakuru lodge have your lunch and later evening game drive at 1600hrs. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

    Day 2

    Maasai Mara National Reserve

    0630hrs breakfast and check out the lake Nakuru national park ,the 5hrs drive journey will take you southwest of Kenya where is located the great Maasai Mara national reserve also sharing boundaries with Serengeti National park in Tanzania.The vast plain of grassland and shrubs trees offers a unique scenery, as the predictors and the prey mingle and graze on the vast green fields.
    Check in at Ol-Moran tented camp later in the day have lunch and evening game drive later 1600hrs. Dinner and overnight at Ol-Moran camp.

    Day 3

    Full day game drive in Maasai Mara game reserve

    0700hrs after breakfast you’ll leave the camp for a full day game drive, picnic lunch will be served at bush. The guests are expected to see wide range of wildlife the area being highly populated by lions, cheetah and leopard.
    Later in the evening drive back to the camp for dinner and overnight.

    Day 4

    Amboseli National Park

    0630hrs breakfast and check out the camp, the 8hrs drive journey will take you to Amboseli national park situated at the root of Mt Kilimanjaro the highest peak in the continent Africa. It’s mostly attractive for it’s beautiful landscape surrounded by wooded and bushy grassland. The park is a great habitat for large herds of black elephants, lions, antelopes and zebras.
    Later in the day check in at Kibo safari camp, have a late lunch and game drive at 1600hrs. Dinner and overnight at Kibo.

    Day 5

    full day game drive in the park

    0700hrs leave the camp for a full day game drive the great opportunity to enjoy wildlife and the magnificent nature across the land. picnic lunch will be served at the bush and later in the evening drive back to the camp for dinner and overnight.

    Day 6

    Tsavo East National Park

    0630hrs breakfast and check out at the camp the 6hrs journey Will take you south east of Kenya where is located Tsavo east national park. Famously known for its beautiful rugged wilderness offering one of the most magnificent game viewing , having great population of red elephants , buffaloes , lions and giraffes.
    Check in at voi safari lodge have lunch and evening game drive at 1600hrs . Dinner and overnight at voi safari lodge.

    Day 7

    Journey back to hotels in Mombasa or Diani

    You’ll have Early breakfast and check out the lodge and enjoy a short hours game drive on your way out of the park and the journey will take you to Mombasa or Diani for your next face of holiday at beach.
