    Day 1

    Amboseli National Park

    pick up from your hotel in Nairobi or Mombasa and drive to Amboseli national park Kenyan most visited park notorious for its incredible view of the jungle crowned by Mt Kilimanjaro. Amboseli has a great population of the big African elephant, cape buffaloes, zebras and giraffes.
    Later in the day check in at Kibo safari camp for lunch and later evening game drive at 1600hrs. -Dinner and overnight at Kibo safari camp .

    Day 2

    whole day game drive in the park

    Early breakfast at 0630hrs and check out the camp for a whole day game drive. picnic lunch served at the bush and later in the evening drive back to the camp for refreshment and prepared for the next day.

    Tsavo West National Park

    0900hrs breakfast and Check out at the camp , 3hrs drive to Tsavo west national park and later in the day check in at Serena lodge kilanguni or Taita hills safari lodge. evening game drive later at 1600hrs.
