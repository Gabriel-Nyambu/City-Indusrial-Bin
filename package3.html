    Day 1

    Ol Pejeta Wildlife Conservancy

    0700hrs pick up from your hotel in Nairobi and the 4hrs 30min journey will take you to 0l Pejeta conservancy situated northern Kenya and at the foothills of Mt Kenya and Aberdare Mt. It’s the only place in Kenya where you can see all the big 5 and chimpanzee. Later in the day check in at sweat waters camp have lunch and wait for the evening game drive at 1600hrs. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

    Day 2

    Lake Nakuru National Park

    0630hrs breakfast and check out the camp the journey will take you to Lake Nakuru National Park, notorious for its great number of flamingoes and pelicans. Hosting over 100 species of migratory birds as well as Zebras, Buffaloes and other predators. Check in at lion hill lodge, have your lunch and later evening Game drive at 1600hrs Dinner and overnight at lion hill lodge.

    Day 3

    Full day game drive in Maasai Mara game reserve

    0630hrs breakfast and checkout at lion hill lodge, 4hour drive to Maasai Mara national reserve situated southern part of Kenya and also consists the northern stretch of the Serengeti National park in Tanzania. It is best known for its massive concentration of animals and the panoramic view of the vast savanna grassland covered by scattered vegetation and distanced hills. The conservancy holds largest population of lions and leopard in Kenya. Later in the day check in at Ol-Moran tented camp have your lunch and later evening game drive at 1600 hours. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

    Day 4

    Full Day Game Drive at Mara Game Reserve

    After breakfast you will leave the camp for full day game drive in the park, its only here you’ll witness the bizarre moments, the prey grazing on the same field with the predator and watch large families of hippos and crocodile at Mara river. Picnic lunch will be served in the bush during the day and later get back to the camp at 1600hrs. Dinner and overnight at Ol-Moran camp.

    Day 5

    Amboseli National Park

    0630hrs breakfast and check out the camp, an eight (8) hours’ drive to Amboseli National Park. Crowned by Mt. Kilimanjaro the highest peak in Africa, Amboseli is popularly known for its large number of black African elephants, cape buffaloes, lions and diverse species of birds.Check in at Setrim Camp Amboseli and later in the day at 1600hrs evening game drive and back to the camp for dinner and overnight.

    Day 6

    Full day game drive in Amboseli National Park

    0700hrs leave the camp for a full day game drive, visit the Olkenya swamp serving as source of water for many animals in the park as well as being the best place for bird watching, hosting hundreds of different species of birds and the most amazing part of it is the close view of the elephant and white egrets feeding in the swamps. Picnic lunch served at the bush and later in the evening, drive back to the camp for dinner and overnight.

    Day 7

    Tsavo East National Park

    0630hrs, breakfast and check out the camp. The journey will lead you to the most visited park in Kenya. Tsavo East National Park, for its great population of red elephants, lions and cheetahs. However, its serenity and the breathtaking panoramic view of the hills and savannah grassland make it outstanding. Later in the day check in at Voi Safari Lodge for an evening game drive. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

    Back to Nairobi Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA)

    0630hrs, check out the hotel have a morning game drive way out the park. 8hrs drive to Nairobi, Jomo Kenyatta International Airport for flight departure.
